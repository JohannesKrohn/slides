Frontify

What does it do?

It is a mixture of confluence, jira and zeplin. It cannot not replace jira or confluence but works well alongside both.

Filling the project space

One of the things that Frontify provides that for example zeplin and figma are missing is a project space where you can also host files. That is convenient for handover and a detailed briefing.

  • Journey Maps
  • Development ready assets
  • Additional or detailed guidances
  • Secondary Studies
  • Deep links to comments

Jakob’s Law

Users spend most of their time on other sites. This means that users prefer your site to work the same way as all the other sites they already know.

– Jakob Nielsen

ESPresso

Marc Daseking, Henning Block and Anish Nagaraj reached out to us for UX / UI Design support

The Espresso platform intends to create a digitally enabled advised and non-advised self-service portal for teams to help themselves in resolving or fulfilling different incidents which is otherwise taken care by an EPS representative or a guided Gitlab CI pipeline.
